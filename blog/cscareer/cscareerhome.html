My First Internship

Just as the school year was about half way up, I was beginning to lose hope in terms of getting an internship. It was towards the end of Winter quarter (Feb ~ Mar) when my cousin contacted me, suggesting that I should apply for an internship at Genentech, and I will forever by grateful that she did.

The other interns I had the pleasure to meet were all very talented and diverse. There were people coming from up and down California, and even one from the east coast. Definitely an experience I will not forget.

In this blog post, I wanted to share my experiences leading up to the internship, the internship itself, and what my next moves are. Without further ado, here it is.
