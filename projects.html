Magic Mirror

Ever since watching this video and this video back in Jan 2017, I've been wanting to make my own smart mirror. I love the idea of finding creative and useful ways to integrate technology into our daily lives. So here we are.

Because this project has a mixture of DIY hardware and software, I decided it would be the perfect opportunity to take on this project with my dad! He would help me cut out frames / work the saws, and I would handle all the software (the best part).

My intention for this blog post is to share my experiences with building this project. Additionally, I wanted it to become a resource for whoever wanted to make something like this! So without further ado, here it is!
